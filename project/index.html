Callmax SEO Master Plan

Executive Overview

We win organic buyers by focusing on bottom funnel searches and a clean interlinked structure. The foundation is two hubs, Services and Industries, supported by Pricing, Comparisons, Integrations, and Resources. We publish money pages first, then expand into high value verticals and proof content.

Why this wins

  • Captures people already shopping for outsourcing
  • Dual hubs build breadth and depth for topical authority
  • Support hubs serve evaluators who want costs, comparisons, and platform know how

Optional enhancements, budget dependent

  • Cost estimator and coverage planner on key pages
  • Honest alternatives and versus pages
  • Integration playbooks for Zendesk, Gorgias, Shopify, and WooCommerce

Note, keywords shown later are examples. We will finalize during research.
